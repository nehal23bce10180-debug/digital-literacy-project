Social Media Do’s:
1. Keep your profile professional.
2. Share useful and positive content.
3. Respect others online.
4. Use strong privacy settings.
5. Think before posting.

Social Media Don’ts:
1. Do not share personal information.
2. Avoid posting offensive content.
3. Do not spread fake news.
4. Avoid oversharing daily activities.
5. Do not use weak passwords.
