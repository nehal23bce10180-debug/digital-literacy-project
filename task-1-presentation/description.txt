This infographic was created as part of Task 1 of the Digital Literacy project using Canva. The objective of the design is to spread awareness among students about the importance of digital literacy in today’s academic and professional environment.

The infographic covers key areas such as the definition of digital literacy, safe internet practices, professional online presence, email etiquette, and cyber safety. Each section highlights essential points in a simple and visually engaging format so that it can be easily understood by students.

While creating this infographic, I focused on keeping the design clean, using minimal text, relevant icons, and proper color combinations to improve readability. One challenge I faced was organizing multiple topics within a single page without making it look crowded. However, by using a structured layout and Canva templates, I was able to present the information effectively.

This task helped me understand not only the concept of digital literacy but also the importance of presenting information in a clear and visually appealing way.
This infographic can be used as a quick reference guide for students to practice safe and responsible digital behavior.
