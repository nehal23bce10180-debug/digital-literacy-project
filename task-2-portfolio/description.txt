This task involved creating a digital portfolio using platforms such as GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. The purpose of this task was to understand how students can build a professional online presence and showcase their skills effectively.

GitHub is used to store and manage coding projects, allowing users to share their work and collaborate with others. LinkedIn is a professional networking platform where students can create profiles, add their education details, and connect with professionals. Kaggle is a platform mainly used for data science and machine learning practice, where users can work on datasets and improve their analytical skills.

While completing this task, I learned the importance of maintaining a clean and professional digital identity. I understood that these platforms are not just for creating accounts but for continuously updating skills, projects, and achievements.

In the next four years, I plan to actively use these platforms by adding new projects, participating in competitions, and improving my skills. This will help me build a strong digital portfolio and increase my career opportunities in the future.
This task helped me realize the importance of authenticity and consistency in building a digital presence.
