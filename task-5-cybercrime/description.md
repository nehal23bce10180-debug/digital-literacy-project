## Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness (Phishing)

In this task, I studied phishing, a common type of cybercrime where attackers try to trick users into revealing sensitive information such as passwords, OTPs, and bank details by impersonating trusted organizations.

I created a detailed case study explaining how phishing attacks typically occur, including steps like sending fake emails or messages, redirecting users to fraudulent websites, and collecting their confidential data. I also described the common targets and the serious consequences of such attacks, including financial loss and identity theft.

In addition, I prepared a prevention checklist that includes practical safety measures such as verifying email sources, avoiding suspicious links, enabling two-factor authentication, and reporting incidents through official cybercrime portals.

This task improved my understanding of online security risks and helped me learn how to identify and prevent phishing attacks effectively.
